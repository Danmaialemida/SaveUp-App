Getting Started

Up and running in minutes.

1

Admin creates the group

Download the app, choose "Create a Group", name it (Home, Finance, etc.) and get your invite code.

2

Family joins with code or QR

Other members enter the 6-char code or scan the QR, set their name and emoji — they're in.

3

Sync & manage together

On the same Wi-Fi, all devices sync instantly. Track budgets, transactions and goals together.
