# SaveUp - Privacy-First Financial Management 🛡️

**SaveUp** is a local-first, privacy-focused financial management tool for families and small businesses. We believe your financial data belongs to you, which is why everything stays on your device.

## Key Features
- **Local-First Architecture**: Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly sync it.
- **Wi-Fi Sync**: Sync with family or team members over your local network—no cloud required.
- **Subscription Models**: Scalable plans for individuals, couples, families, and businesses.
- **Advanced Business Tools**: P&L reporting, Cost Centers, and tax compliance (CRA/GST/PST support).
- **Privacy by Design**: No accounts, no trackers, no cloud servers.
